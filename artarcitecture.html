Art & Arcitecture

Montréal is known for having great street art. If you are a fan of artists such as Banksy, you will love Montréal. It's hard to find a building in the city without some sort of street art on it. Come to our info center and we'll give you a free tour of all the great tours that will show you all the best street art that Montréal has to offer!

Here are some well known Artists from Montréal:

  • Celia Perrin Sidarous
  • Josée Pedneault
  • Lynne Marsh
  • Isabelle Hayeur
  • Michel de Broin
