Neighborhoods

There are lots of great neighborhoods in Monréal! We have a very low crime rate, a high standard of living, low poverty, and housing prices are at an all time low! Employment here is at 99.9%, so you are sure to find a job, no matter what kind of skills you have to offer.

History

Monréal has a very rich history. Canada, for those of you that don't know, has a lot of French people. This is because a lot of people that live here are have ancestors from France! Want to learn more about the awesome histroy of Monréal? Come visit some of our museums and information centers!

Art & Arcitecture

Monréal is known for having great street art. If you are a fan of artists such as Banksy, you will love Monréal. It's hard to find a building in the city without some sort of street art on it. Come to our info center and we'll give you a free tour of all the great tours that will show you all the best street art that Monréal has to offer!

Must See Sites

Here in Monréal, everywhere is a must see site. You can't really go wrong with just walking throughout the city and taking in the views. Just make sure to bring some warm clothes if it's Fall or Winter!

Food & Restaurants

Monréal has the best food in the world. We have a diverse selection of French food, Canadian food, and even American food. Of course you can find whatever genre of food that you like, those are just our specialties.
